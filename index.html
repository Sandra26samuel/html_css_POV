What Is POV ?

A Point Of view (POV) is a meaningful and actionable problem statement, which will allow you to ideate in a goal-oriented manner.

USER

In startup development, understanding the target user is paramount. This involves gaining insights into their demographics, behaviors, preferences, and pain points.

Example: Suppose a startup is developing a mobile fitness app. Understanding the user might involve identifying their age range (e.g., young adults), fitness goals (e.g., weight loss, muscle gain), preferred workout styles (e.g., yoga, HIIT), and typical usage patterns (e.g., morning workouts, evening routines).

→

Needs

Identifying and addressing the needs of users is essential for creating a product or service that provides genuine value and meets market demand.

Example: Continuing with the fitness app example, the needs of users might include access to diverse workout routines, tracking progress and achievements, receiving personalized recommendations based on fitness goals, and connecting with a supportive community of like-minded individuals.

→

Susrprising Insights

Insights refer to unexpected or valuable observations gained through user research, data analysis, or real-world experiences. These insights can inform strategic decisions and drive innovation.

Example: While testing the fitness app with users, the startup team discovers that a significant portion of users prefer short, high-intensity workouts during lunch breaks. This surprising insight could prompt the team to prioritize the development of quick workout sessions tailored for busy professionals.
